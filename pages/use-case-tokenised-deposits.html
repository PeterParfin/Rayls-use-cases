Use Case

Tokenised Deposits

The programmable foundation for institutional blockchain. Mint, transfer, and extend fiat deposits as EVM-standard tokens within a sovereign Rayls Privacy Node, preserving the bank's balance sheet while unlocking new utility.

Sovereign Privacy Node Balance Sheet Preserving EVM-Compatible Institutional Grade

The Problem

Batch-driven infrastructure traps deposits and blocks programmability

Compliance costs alone have surged over 60% since the financial crisis (Deloitte), while batch settlement cycles lock intraday capital and prevent deposits from participating in programmable workflows.

Status quo

Deposits trapped in siloed core banking systems. Reconciliation across general ledger, treasury, and risk runs in end-of-day batch cycles.
Internal desk-to-desk transfers require multiple ledger updates, manual netting, and T+1 or later settlement. Intraday liquidity buffers absorb the timing gaps.
Compliance and access controls sit in middleware layers disconnected from the deposit record, increasing regulatory risk and audit overhead.
No programmable pathway to extend deposit utility into external settlement networks, cross-border rails, or yield-generating environments.

With Rayls

Deposits minted as ERC-20 tokens on the bank's Privacy Node with automated reconciliation across core banking, general ledger, and treasury. Single source of truth.
Atomic internal transfers settle with sub-second finality. DvP smart contracts settle against other tokenised assets risk-free, eliminating netting cycles and intraday buffers.
Eligibility, limits, and access controls embedded in the token's smart contract and enforced onchain with a complete audit trail.
Cryptographic stablecoin bridging extends deposit utility to the Rayls Public Chain for treasury management, inter-institutional trading, cross-border payments, and yield distribution.

The Solution

Rayls Privacy Node

Fiat deposits become programmable ERC-20 tokens within a sovereign, on-premises blockchain with full core banking integration and automated compliance.

Programmable Deposit Issuance

Mint tokenised deposits 1:1 against fiat with role-based access controls and automated reconciliation across core banking, general ledger, and treasury systems.

Atomic Internal Settlement

Sub-second finality for desk-to-desk transfers. DvP smart contracts settle tokenised deposits against other assets risk-free, with zero settlement gaps.

Embedded Compliance and Audit

Eligibility, limits, and access controls encoded in the token's smart contract. Every issuance, transfer, and redemption produces a verifiable onchain audit trail.

Stablecoin Bridging and Yield Extension

Lock tokenised deposits to mint stablecoins on the Rayls Public Chain. Access global liquidity, DeFi yield, and cross-border payment rails while preserving the bank's deposit base.

Tokenised deposits are the foundation layer. Once deposits are programmable, they serve as the base asset for treasury management and internal trading, private multi-institution asset settlement, cross-border stablecoin payments, and yield-bearing asset vaults. Every subsequent use case builds on this infrastructure.
