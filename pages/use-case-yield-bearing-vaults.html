Use Case

Yield-Bearing Asset Vaults

Tokenise regulated assets privately, then distribute yield exposure to qualified global investors through on-chain vault structures on the Rayls Public Chain.

Private-to-Public Distribution Curated Vault Management Compliant Investor Access Composable Yield

The Problem

Yield-bearing assets are trapped behind local infrastructure

Regulated institutions hold illiquid, yield-generating assets that cannot reach global investor demand. Legacy distribution locks capital in place and leaves billions in potential allocation on the table.

Status quo

Yield-bearing instruments remain confined to domestic investor bases with no programmable pathway to global capital pools.
Cross-border distribution requires separate legal vehicles, local agents and jurisdiction-specific filings, adding months and cost to each new market.
Investor onboarding takes days to weeks. 74% of Tier 1 asset managers have lost clients due to slow or inefficient onboarding processes.
Issued assets sit illiquid on balance sheets, tying up working capital that could be redeployed or used as collateral for further investment.

With Rayls

On-chain vault structures serve as the compliant distribution layer, connecting locally regulated assets to qualified global investors across public blockchain infrastructure.
A single tokenised asset, issued privately and bridged once to the Public Chain, reaches global investors without local intermediaries or duplicate filings.
Smart-contract-enforced identity verification and whitelisting at the vault level. Compliant wallets interact instantly, non-compliant wallets are blocked programmatically.
Tokenised assets deposited into vaults unlock liquidity immediately. Vault deposit tokens are composable and can be reused as collateral, freeing capital for reallocation.

The Solution

Rayls Privacy Node + Public Chain

Tokenise assets privately, bridge them compliantly, and distribute yield to qualified global investors via curated on-chain vaults.

Private Origination, Public Distribution

Tokenise regulated assets within a Privacy Node, lock them as collateral, and bridge public-chain equivalents via the Private Bridge for global distribution.

Curated Vault Management

Curators define strategy, risk providers score assets on-chain, and oracles feed real-time net asset value data. Vault contracts enforce limits and rebalancing automatically.

Compliant Global Capital Access

Identity and suitability checks enforced at the smart contract level. Qualified investors deposit stablecoins (e.g. USDC, USDT, USDr) and receive composable vault deposit tokens.

Layered Yield and Capital Recycling

Investors receive yield from underlying assets plus optional lending pool returns. Vault deposit tokens can be reused as collateral, freeing capital for reallocation across investment strategies.
