Cross-Border Payments & FX

Yield-bearing tokenised deposits with instant cross-border stablecoin settlement, replacing correspondent banking with transparent, privacy-preserving institutional transfers.

The Problem

Idle deposits, trapped liquidity and multi-day settlement

Client deposits earn below-market rates. Cross-border payments route through 2 to 5 correspondent banks over 2 to 5 business days, with hidden fees and FX markups at every hop.

Status quo

Standard deposit accounts pay well below the risk-free rate. The spread between treasury yields and deposit rates is captured entirely by the bank.
Each payment passes through 2 to 5 correspondent banks, each deducting $25 to $75 per hop. Beneficiaries receive less than sent, with no upfront visibility of deductions.
Settlement takes 2 to 5 business days across time zones, weekends and holidays. Only 35% of retail cross-border payments credit within one hour (FSB/BIS, 2025).
Banks pre-fund nostro accounts in every currency corridor, locking capital at minimal return. Average annual cost per bank: $1.5 million (Visa, 2024).

With Rayls

Clients convert deposits into treasury-backed yield-bearing tokenised deposits, earning closer to the risk-free rate while the bank retains a management fee.
Direct institution-to-institution stablecoin transfer via the Rayls Public Chain. No intermediary chain. One on-ramp fee, one off-ramp fee, full end-to-end transparency.
Near-instant settlement, 24/7. Stablecoin transfers reach deterministic finality in seconds, irrespective of time zones or banking hours.
On-demand stablecoin liquidity eliminates pre-funded nostro accounts. Capital freed for productive deployment.

The Solution

Rayls Privacy Node and Public Chain with Enygma

Yield-bearing tokenised deposits within the bank's Privacy Node, combined with stablecoin-settled cross-border transfers via the Rayls Public Chain and Enygma privacy protocol.

Yield-Bearing Tokenised Deposits

Clients convert standard deposits into treasury-backed tokenised deposits within the Privacy Node, earning a share of the government securities yield while the bank retains a management fee.

Atomic On-Ramp via HTLC

For cross-border payments, the bank swaps tokenised deposits with a local LP for USD stablecoin via a Hash Time-Locked Contract. Either both legs complete or neither does.

Private Cross-Border Stablecoin Transfer

Enygma on the Rayls Public Chain enables institutions to transfer USD stablecoins cross-border in seconds, with amounts and counterparties cryptographically shielded from uninvolved parties.

Auditable Compliance and Regulator Access

The Enygma dApp operator can grant regulators auditor view keys or read-only access under local law. Transfers are fully traceable on-chain while private from uninvolved participants.
